    Sync (via iCloud Drive / Dropbox / any synced folder)

    Pick a sync file in any cloud-synced folder (iCloud Drive, Dropbox, Google Drive, OneDrive). Your data writes there on every change. Open this app on another device, point it at the same file, and you're synced — no account, no server, no email. Works in Chrome and Edge.
    Tip: navigate to ~/Library/Mobile Documents/com~apple~CloudDocs/ (iCloud Drive) and save as vesper-sync.json. Then on your iPhone/iPad/other Mac, browse to the same file.
